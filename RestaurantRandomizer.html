Restaurant
Randomizer

Android Mobile application

This Project is still under construction. The idea behind it is simple. How many times have your partner and yourself being undecisive about where to eat? No one have time for those kind of arguments, and that is what this app is for! You could enter a city or zipcode, and it will randomly choose a restaurant for you.

The application uses the Yelp API. Currently, the application is only making a simple call for a list of restaurants in the area. It shows the name, stars, dollar amount and what kind of cruisine of the restaurant. It will give more information when you click into it.
